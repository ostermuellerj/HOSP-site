A digital archive of the beetles of HSNP.

Select a category to begin. Old beetles are from the Park's original 1960s archives, New beetles have been collected as a part of this project. Invasive beetles currently (or did at one point in time) pose a threat to the Park's native species and ecosystem. The taxonomic identifications of each specimen are listed when hovered over. Beetles with abreviation "sp." are not identified to species level. Beetles with a "?" are yet to be identified. Click the thumbnails to see additional photos of each beetle.
